# Epic Global Inc. Website Redesign - Complete Summary

## Overview
Complete website redesign for Epic Global Inc. - a premium, tech-forward, dark-mode eCommerce operations platform. The site is built for conversion, clarity, and credibility.

## Design System Implemented

### Visual Design
- **Dark Mode Default**: Black background (#000000) with glassmorphism panels
- **Glassmorphism**: Frosted glass panels with `backdrop-blur-xl` and subtle borders
- **Neon Cyan Accents**: Cyan-500/600 used sparingly for CTAs and interactive elements
- **Typography**: Clear hierarchy with oversized headings and clean subheads
- **Shadows**: Soft shadows with cyan glow effects on hover states
- **Mobile-First**: Fully responsive design optimized for all devices

### Brand Voice
- Confident, analytical, concise
- Operator mindset (not agency/marketing speak)
- No buzzword soup or generic marketing language
- Clear, direct copy focused on execution and outcomes

## Three-Pillar Model Integration

Throughout the site, the three pillars are consistently reinforced:

1. **PURCHASE**: We purchase inventory upfront at wholesale pricing
2. **EXPAND**: Repeatable launch system for global marketplace expansion
3. **COVER**: We cover marketplace fees, advertising, and fulfillment costs

This model is:
- Introduced on Home (prominent placement)
- Explained on About
- Demonstrated in Case Studies (filterable by pillar)
- Referenced in Services
- Integrated into How It Works

## Key Differentiators Highlighted

1. **Operator, not agency**: We execute, not advise
2. **Inventory ownership**: Aligned incentives through wholesale purchase
3. **Expense coverage**: We fund ads, fees, fulfillment
4. **System-driven**: Repeatable processes, not one-off tactics
5. **Global by design**: Built for multi-market scale
6. **Compliance-first**: Risk management built into workflows
7. **Data transparency**: SKU-level P&L and attribution
